Potatoes Pork

Pork Ribs with Potatoes is a traditional, hearty dish where tender pork ribs are slowly stewed with potatoes, garlic, onions, and spices, creating a rich and comforting sauce full of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Pork ribs
  • Potatoes
  • Garlic cloves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Preparation Steps:

  1. Season the pork ribs with salt, black pepper, and paprika.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  3. Brown the ribs on all sides, then remove and set aside.
  4. In the same pot, saut√© the chopped onion and garlic until softened.
  5. Return the ribs to the pot and add the bay leaf.
  6. Add water or stock to cover the ingredients.
  7. Simmer gently until the ribs are tender.
  8. Add the potatoes and continue cooking until they are soft and the sauce has thickened slightly.
  9. Adjust seasoning and serve hot.
