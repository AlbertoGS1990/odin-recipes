Beer Chicken

Beer Chicken is a comforting, savory dish where chicken is slowly cooked in beer with onions, garlic, and aromatic herbs. The beer adds depth and a slightly malty flavor, while the meat becomes tender and juicy, coated in a rich, flavorful sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken pieces
  • Beer
  • Onion
  • Salt

Preparation Steps:

  1. Season the chicken pieces with salt.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan or pot over medium heat.
  3. Brown the chicken on all sides, then remove and set aside
  4. In the same pan, saut√© the sliced onion until soft and fragrant.
  5. Return the chicken to the pan and add the bay leaf and herbs.
  6. Pour in the beer until the chicken is partially covered.
  7. Lower the heat, cover, and let it simmer gently until the chicken is tender and the sauce has thickened slightly.
  8. Adjust seasoning if needed and serve hot.
