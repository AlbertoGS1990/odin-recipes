Cuban-style Rice

Arroz a la Cubana is a simple and popular dish made with fluffy white rice served with a fried egg and fried plantain, often accompanied by tomato sauce. It is quick, affordable, and very comforting.

Ingredients:

  • White rice
  • Eggs
  • Ripe plantain
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Preparation Steps:

  1. Cook the white rice in salted water until tender and fluffy.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the plantain slices until golden; set aside.
  3. In the same pan, fry the eggs to your liking.
  4. Heat the tomato sauce gently.
  5. Serve the rice topped with the fried egg, alongside the fried plantain and tomato sauce.
